Chaque image de décoration sans légende (balise img) et ayant un attribut alt vérifie-t-elle ces conditions :
  • le contenu de l'attribut alt est vide (alt="")
  • l'image de décoration ne possède pas d'attribut title

Passed: One image is marked as decorative and its alt attribute is empty. The other images are either an image link, either an image with a longdesc attribute, either an image marked as informative or an image without alt attribute. They are all out of scope.
